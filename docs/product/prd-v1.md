# AgentOps Product Requirements Document (PRD)

Version: 1.0
Status: Draft

---

# Product Name

AgentOps

---

# Product Category

AI Workforce Platform for African Businesses

---

# Product Vision

AgentOps enables businesses to deploy AI Employees that execute business work across existing tools such as email, messaging platforms, documents, spreadsheets, CRMs, ERPs and cloud storage.

Unlike traditional AI assistants that answer questions, AgentOps performs tasks, coordinates workflows and collaborates with people.

---

# Problem Statement

Businesses lose opportunities because information is fragmented.

Customer conversations happen in WhatsApp.

Sales enquiries arrive through email.

Documents are stored in Google Drive.

Invoices are created in Excel.

Tasks are remembered by people instead of systems.

There is no intelligent worker coordinating these activities.

---

# Target Customers

Primary Market

African Small and Medium Businesses (SMEs)

Examples include:

- Technology companies
- Consulting firms
- Training organizations
- Marketing agencies
- Recruitment firms
- NGOs
- Churches
- Law firms
- Accounting firms
- Real estate companies

---

# Core Value Proposition

Deploy AI Employees that execute work rather than simply answering questions.

---

# MVP

The first release focuses on one AI Employee.

Opportunity Agent

Purpose:

Capture business opportunities from multiple channels and help businesses convert them into paying customers.

---

# Success Metrics

- Faster response to enquiries
- More proposals generated
- Higher opportunity conversion
- Reduced manual administration
- Improved business productivity

---

# Out of Scope

The MVP will not include:

- HR Agent
- Finance Agent
- Procurement Agent
- Customer Support Agent
- Voice AI
- Mobile App
- Marketplace

These will be introduced in future releases.
