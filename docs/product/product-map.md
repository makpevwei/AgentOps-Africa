# AgentOps Product Map

Version: 1.0

---

# Platform

AgentOps is an AI Workforce Platform that enables businesses to deploy AI Employees capable of executing business work.

Every AI Employee shares the same platform.

- Authentication
- Organizations
- Users
- Knowledge Base
- Memory
- Workflow Engine
- Reasoning Engine
- AI Providers
- Integrations
- Documents
- Activity Feed
- Notifications
- Audit Logs

---

# AI Employees

## Opportunity Agent

Purpose

Convert enquiries into paying customers.

Responsibilities

- Capture enquiries
- Research prospects
- Generate proposals
- Generate quotations
- Schedule follow-ups
- Track opportunities

---

Purpose

Responsibilities

- Email
- Meeting summaries
- Task management
- Reminders
- Decision support
